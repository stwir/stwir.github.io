SHOAS (pronounced "Shaw-as") coming soon... 
The project is concerned about spotforming by combining multiple Higher-Order Ambisonics (HOA) signals captured by distributed microphone arrays. The goal is to enhance a target sound source while suppressing interference from other directions, using different orders of HOA receivers.
